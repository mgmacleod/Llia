SnH Sample And Hold

Home Contents Synth Catalog Previous: LFO3 None

SnH is a Sample And Hold control synth which produces a wide range of stepped and random signals. The sampling clock may be either internal or external with three sources of sampled signal:

  1. Internal sawtooth LFO
  2. Internal white noise generator
  3. External control signal

Clock

The sampling clock may be either internal or external.
Clock, tumbler, sets internal clock frequency.
Internal/External button, select clock source.

Source

The three sliders selects the mix of the sampled signal. The Sawtooth and Noise sliders are for internal sources. The Sawtooth tumbler sets sawtooth frequency.

Out

Lag, smoothing of output signal.
Scale, factor applied to output signal.
Bias, fixed value added to output signal.
