SS2 (Simple Synth 2) is a bare bones instrument.

  • Single multi-wave oscillator:
    • Sawtooth
    • Pulse (50%, 25%, 12%, 6%)
    • Triangle.
    Sawtooth and Pulse waves are band-limited. The Triangle is formed by wave shaping the Sawtooth and is not band-limited.
  • Single multi-mode filter:
    • Bypass
    • Lowpass
    • Highpass
    The filter always tracks the keyboard by a selected ratio.
  • Dual mode amplitude Envelope:
    • ADSR
    • Percussive
