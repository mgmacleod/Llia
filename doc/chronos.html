Dual delay line

Delay 1 / Delay 2

Delays 1 and 2 are identical except that Delay 1 may be shunted to Delay 2 input.

  • Input Mix A - Dry signal A input mix
  • Input Mix B - Dry signal B input mix
  • Delay 1 - (Delay 2 only) Delay 1 amount sent to delay 2
  • Delay Time - tumbler in milliseconds
  • LFO Ratio - button, LFO frequency ratio
  • LFO - Delay time modulation be LFO
  • Extern - Delay time modulation by external signal
  • Low - Lowpass filter cutoff
  • High - Highpass filter cutoff

Output Mixer

  • A - Dry signal A output mix
  • B - Dry signal B output mix
  • Delay 1 - Delay 1 wet signal output mix
  • Delay 2 - Delay 2 wet signal output mix
  • Pan A
  • Pan B
  • Pan Delay 1
  • Pan Delay 2

LFO

The LFO tumbler sets reference frequency in Hertz. The frequencies of Delay 1 LFO and Delay 2 LFO are relative to this value. The small button to the right of the tumbler sets delay time to an integer value between 1 and 7.

Buses

  • inbus1 - Audio input 1(A)
  • inbus2 - Audio input 2(B)
  • outbus1 - Audio output 1
  • outbus2 - Audio output 2
  • xbus - External control input

Parameters

  • lfoCommonFreq - LFO reference frequency (0 ... 16.999)
  • d1Dry1In - Delay 1 input 1(A) mix (0 ... 2)
  • d1Dry2In - Delay 1 input 2(B) mix (0 ... 2)
  • d1DelayTime - Delay time 1 in seconds (0 ... 2)
  • d1LfoRatio - Delay 1 LFO ratio (0.125,0.25,0.5,0.75,1,1.333,1.5,2,2.5,3,4,5,6,8)
  • d1LfoModDepth - Delay 1 LFO modulation depth (0 ... 1)
  • d1ExternalModDepth - Delay 1 external modulation depth (0 ... 1)
  • d1Feedback - Delay 1 feedback (-1 ... +1)
  • d1Lowpass - Delay 1 lowpass cutoff (20 ... 20k)
  • d1Highpass - Delay 1 highpass cutoff (20 ... 20k)
  • d2Dry1In - Delay 2 input 1(A) mix (0 ... 2)
  • d2Dry2In - Delay 2 input 2(B) mix (0 ... 2)
  • d2Delay1In - Delay 2 input mix from delay 1 (0 ... 2)
  • d2DelayTime - Delay time 2 in seconds (0 ... 2)
  • d2LfoRatio - Delay 2 LFO ratio (0.125,0.25,0.5,0.75,1,1.333,1.5,2,2.5,3,4,5,6,8)
  • d2LfoModDepth - Delay 2 LFO modulation depth (0 ... 1)
  • d2ExternalModDepth - Delay 2 external modulation depth (0 ... 1)
  • d2Feedback - Delay 2 feedback (-1 ... +1)
  • d2Lowpass - Delay 2 lowpass cutoff (20 ... 20k)
  • d2Highpass - Delay 2 highpass cutoff (20 ... 20k)
  • dry1Amp - Dry signal 1(A) output mix (0 ... 2)
  • dry2Amp - Dry signal 2(B) output mix (0 ... 2)
  • d1Amp - Delay 1 output mix (0 ... 2)
  • d2Amp - Delay 2 output mix (0 ... 2)
  • dry1Pan - Dry 1(A) pan (-1 ... +1)
  • dry2Pan - Dry 2(B) pan (-1 ... +1)
  • d1Pan - Delay 1 pan (-1 ... +1)
  • d2Pan - Delay 2 pan (-1 ... +1)
