Creating an Instrument

  1. From the main window make sure the "synths" tab is selected.
  1. Click an available instruments.
  2. After the "add-instrument" dialog appears, accept the defaults and click "OK".

The dialog is replaced with a new group window with a single active synth. At this point the instrument should be playable on MIDI channel 1.

To view an editor for this synth click one of the tabs to the right of the "Info" tab.
